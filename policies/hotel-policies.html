Hotel Policies & Regulations

Last Updated: December 2024

To ensure a pleasant and safe stay for all our guests, Hotel Ortus has established the following policies and guidelines. We appreciate your cooperation in adhering to these regulations.

1. Check-In & Check-Out

1.1 Check-In

  • Standard Check-in Time: 2:00 PM
  • Early Check-in: Subject to availability, may incur additional charges
  • ID Requirement: Valid government-issued photo ID is mandatory (Aadhaar, PAN Card, Driver's License, Passport)
  • Age Requirement: Guests must be 18 years or older to check-in
  • Advance Booking: Guests with confirmed reservations will be given priority

1.2 Check-Out

  • Standard Check-out Time: 12:00 Noon
  • Late Check-out: Available upon request (subject to availability, charges apply after 2:00 PM)
  • Express Check-out: Bill settlement can be done the night before departure
  • Luggage Storage: Complimentary luggage storage available for checked-out guests

2. Guest Registration & Documentation

2.1 Indian Citizens

  • Valid photo ID must be presented at check-in
  • Guests may be required to fill out a registration form
  • All guests in the room must be registered at the front desk

2.2 Foreign Nationals

  • Passport: Original passport with valid visa required
  • C-Form: Mandatory registration within 24 hours (as per Indian law)
  • Police Verification: Information will be shared with local authorities
  • Copies: Photocopies of passport and visa will be retained

3. Room Occupancy

  • Maximum Occupancy: As per room category (strictly enforced for safety)
  • Extra Guests: Additional charges apply - ₹1000-1500 per person per night
  • Children: Children below 5 years stay free (without extra bedding)
  • Visitors: Visitors must register at the front desk and are not permitted after 10:00 PM
  • Unauthorized Guests: Hosting unregistered guests is prohibited and may result in eviction

4. Payment & Billing

  • Deposit: Refundable security deposit may be required at check-in
  • Payment Methods: Cash, Credit/Debit Cards, UPI, Bank Transfer accepted
  • Advance Payment: Required for confirmed bookings
  • Additional Charges: Room service, minibar, laundry, and damage charges apply
  • GST: All rates are subject to applicable taxes
  • Settlement: Full payment must be cleared before check-out

5. Smoking Policy

  • Non-Smoking Rooms: All guest rooms are non-smoking
  • Designated Areas: Smoking permitted only in designated outdoor areas
  • Violation: ₹5000 cleaning charge for smoking in rooms
  • Fire Safety: Smoking in prohibited areas poses fire hazards and is strictly forbidden

6. Alcohol Policy

  • Consumption of alcohol is permitted only in designated areas
  • Guests must be 18+ years to consume alcohol
  • Outside alcohol is not permitted in public areas or restaurant
  • Intoxicated behavior disturbing other guests will not be tolerated
  • Management reserves the right to refuse service

7. Pet Policy

  • Pets: Not permitted (except service animals with prior approval)
  • Service Animals: Guide dogs and certified service animals are welcome
  • Documentation: Valid certification required for service animals

8. Noise & Conduct

  • Quiet Hours: 10:00 PM - 7:00 AM daily
  • Music: Loud music or TV not permitted during quiet hours
  • Parties: Private gatherings in rooms must not disturb other guests
  • Disturbances: Management may ask guests to leave without refund for disruptive behavior
  • Respect: All guests and staff must be treated with courtesy and respect

9. Safety & Security

9.1 General Safety

  • Emergency exits and fire extinguishers are located on every floor
  • Do not prop open fire doors or emergency exits
  • Follow evacuation procedures in case of fire alarm
  • Report any suspicious activity to security immediately

9.2 Valuables

  • In-Room Safe: Use the in-room safe for valuables
  • Front Desk Deposit: High-value items can be deposited with management
  • Liability: Hotel is not responsible for loss of valuables left in rooms
  • Lost Items: Report lost belongings to reception immediately

9.3 Room Access

  • Do not share room keys with unauthorized persons
  • Always lock your room when leaving
  • Staff will enter rooms only for housekeeping, maintenance, or emergencies
  • Request "Do Not Disturb" service if desired

10. Housekeeping

  • Daily Service: Rooms are cleaned daily between 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
  • Do Not Disturb: Hang sign on door to skip cleaning
  • Towel Change: Hang towels to indicate replacement needed
  • Special Requests: Contact housekeeping for additional amenities
  • Lost & Found: Items left behind are kept for 30 days

11. Damage Policy

  • Guests are responsible for any damage to hotel property
  • Accidental Damage: Report immediately to avoid additional charges
  • Intentional Damage: Full replacement cost + penalty will be charged
  • Missing Items: Cost of missing towels, linens, or amenities will be billed
  • Legal Action: Serious damage may result in police involvement

12. Internet & WiFi

  • Complimentary WiFi: Available in all rooms and public areas
  • Password: Provided at check-in
  • Speed: High-speed broadband connection
  • Usage: Illegal downloads or streaming of prohibited content is forbidden
  • Support: Contact front desk for connectivity issues

13. Parking

  • Free Parking: Complimentary parking available for registered guests
  • Security: Parking is monitored, but hotel is not liable for theft or damage
  • Valet: Valet parking available upon request
  • Vehicle Documents: Keep valid documents in vehicle at all times

14. Food & Beverage

14.1 Dining

  • Restaurant Hours: Breakfast: 7:00 AM - 10:00 AM | Lunch: 12:00 PM - 3:00 PM | Dinner: 7:00 PM - 11:00 PM
  • Room Service: Available 24/7 (limited menu after midnight)
  • Dietary Requirements: Inform staff of allergies or dietary restrictions
  • Outside Food: Not permitted in restaurant or banquet areas

14.2 Food Safety

  • All food is prepared under FSSAI-certified kitchen facilities
  • Hygiene standards are strictly maintained
  • Report any food quality concerns immediately

15. Health & Hygiene (COVID-19 & General)

  • Hand sanitizers available at multiple locations
  • Regular sanitization of high-touch surfaces
  • Guests showing symptoms of illness should inform management
  • Face masks recommended in public areas (as per government guidelines)
  • Social distancing norms to be followed in common areas

16. Banquet & Event Policies

  • Events must be booked in advance with signed agreement
  • Outside decorators require hotel approval
  • Noise limits must be respected (especially after 10:00 PM)
  • Banquet areas must be vacated by the agreed time
  • Damage deposits may be required for large events

17. Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited and may result in immediate eviction without refund:

  • Illegal drug use or possession
  • Prostitution or any unlawful activities
  • Gambling in public areas
  • Weapons or firearms (except authorized personnel)
  • Fireworks or explosive materials
  • Commercial activities without hotel permission

18. Compliance with Indian Laws

Hotel Ortus operates in full compliance with:

  • The Hotels and Lodging Houses Act
  • Food Safety and Standards Act (FSSAI)
  • Local police regulations and verification requirements
  • Fire safety and building codes
  • Rajasthan Tourism regulations
  • Goods and Services Tax (GST) Act

19. Feedback & Complaints

  • We value your feedback - please share your experience
  • Complaints should be reported to management immediately for prompt resolution
  • Feedback forms available at reception
  • Contact our customer service team for post-stay concerns

20. Right to Admission

Hotel Ortus reserves the right to:

  • Refuse admission or service to any person
  • Evict guests violating policies without refund
  • Modify policies with reasonable notice
  • Enter rooms in case of emergency or security concerns

Thank You! We appreciate your cooperation with these policies. Our goal is to ensure every guest enjoys a comfortable, safe, and memorable stay at Hotel Ortus. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact our front desk.
