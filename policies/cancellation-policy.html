Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Important: Please read this policy carefully before making a reservation. By confirming your booking, you acknowledge and accept these cancellation terms.

1. Standard Cancellation Policy

Our cancellation policy varies based on the time of cancellation relative to your check-in date:

Cancellation Time Refund Amount Processing Fee
7+ days before check-in 100% refund ₹500 processing fee
4-6 days before check-in 50% refund ₹500 processing fee
2-3 days before check-in 25% refund No additional fee
Less than 48 hours / No-show No refund N/A

2. Peak Season & Special Events

During peak seasons, festivals, and special events, stricter cancellation terms apply:

  • 14+ days before check-in: 75% refund (₹1000 processing fee)
  • 7-13 days before check-in: 50% refund (₹1000 processing fee)
  • Less than 7 days: No refund

Peak seasons include: Diwali, Holi, New Year, Christmas, and other major Indian festivals. Guests will be informed of peak season status at the time of booking.

3. Group Bookings (5+ Rooms)

Group reservations have different cancellation terms:

  • 30+ days before check-in: 80% refund (₹2000 processing fee)
  • 15-29 days before check-in: 50% refund (₹2000 processing fee)
  • 7-14 days before check-in: 25% refund
  • Less than 7 days: No refund

4. Modification Policy

4.1 Date Changes

  • Date modifications are subject to availability and rate differences
  • One free date change is permitted if requested 7+ days before check-in
  • Additional changes incur a ₹1000 modification fee
  • No modifications allowed within 48 hours of check-in

4.2 Room Type Changes

  • Room upgrades are subject to availability and price difference
  • Downgrades are permitted with 7+ days notice (refund of difference applies)
  • Modification fee: ₹500

5. Refund Processing

5.1 Timeline

  • Refunds are processed within 7-14 business days from the date of cancellation
  • The actual credit to your account depends on your bank or payment provider
  • For cash payments, refunds are issued via bank transfer (provide bank details)

5.2 Method

  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for booking
  • Credit/Debit card refunds: 5-10 business days
  • UPI/Online banking: 3-7 business days
  • Cash/Bank transfer: 7-14 business days

6. How to Cancel

To cancel your reservation, please follow these steps:

  1. Email: Send cancellation request to XXXXXXX
  2. Phone: Call us at XXXXXXX or XXXXXXX
  3. Include: Booking reference number, guest name, and check-in date
  4. Confirmation: You will receive a cancellation confirmation email within 24 hours

7. No-Show Policy

  • If you do not arrive on your scheduled check-in date without prior cancellation, you will be charged the full booking amount
  • No-show charges are 100% of the total booking value
  • In case of delays, please inform the hotel immediately to avoid no-show charges

8. Early Check-Out

  • Early departures are treated as no-shows for remaining nights
  • No refunds are provided for unused nights unless due to hotel's inability to provide services
  • Notify reception immediately if you need to check out early

9. Force Majeure & Special Circumstances

In exceptional circumstances beyond your control, we may offer more flexible cancellation terms:

  • Natural disasters affecting travel to Kota
  • Medical emergencies (doctor's certificate required)
  • Government-imposed travel restrictions
  • Public health emergencies
  • Death in the family (documentation required)

Each case is evaluated individually. Please contact us with supporting documentation for consideration.

10. Banquet & Event Cancellations

Cancellations for banquet hall bookings and events follow different terms:

  • 60+ days before event: 80% refund (₹5000 processing fee)
  • 30-59 days before event: 50% refund (₹5000 processing fee)
  • 15-29 days before event: 25% refund
  • Less than 15 days: No refund

11. Promotional Bookings

  • Bookings made using special offers, discounts, or promotional codes may have different cancellation terms
  • Specific terms will be communicated at the time of booking
  • Some promotional rates may be non-refundable

12. Travel Agent & Third-Party Bookings

  • Reservations made through travel agents or third-party websites are subject to those platforms' cancellation policies
  • Please contact the booking platform directly for cancellations
  • Hotel Ortus's direct cancellation policy applies only to direct bookings

13. Disputes

If you have any concerns regarding cancellation charges or refunds:

  • Contact our customer service team immediately
  • We aim to resolve all disputes within 48 hours
  • For unresolved issues, formal complaints can be submitted in writing
